Personal Security Agreement

1. Introduction

This Personal Security Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between [SYSOIT] (“Provider”) and the user (“User”) who utilizes the G-Contacts app (“App”).

2. Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to ensure that personal data managed or processed by the App is protected against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

3. Definitions

For the purposes of this Agreement:

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Processing: Any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means.
  • Data Subject: The individual to whom the personal data relates.

4. User Responsibilities

The User agrees to:

  • Keep their account credentials confidential and not share them with any unauthorized persons.
  • Promptly report any suspected security incidents or breaches to the Provider.
  • Ensure that any personal data provided to the App is accurate and up-to-date.

5. Provider Responsibilities

The Provider agrees to:

  • Implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.
  • Ensure that any processing of personal data is conducted in compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.
  • Limit access to personal data to authorized personnel who require such access to perform their duties.
  • Provide training to personnel on data protection and information security practices.

6. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may result in a risk to the rights and freedoms of data subjects, the Provider agrees to:

  • Notify the User without undue delay after becoming aware of the breach.
  • Provide information about the nature of the breach, the likely consequences, and the measures taken or proposed to address the breach.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

The Provider agrees to retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, and in accordance with applicable laws and regulations. Upon termination of the User's account, the Provider will securely delete or anonymize the personal data, unless retention is required by law.

8. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of South Korea, without regard to its conflict of law principles.

9. Changes to the Agreement

The Provider reserves the right to modify this Agreement at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on the App or the Provider's website. The User’s continued use of the App following the posting of changes constitutes acceptance of those changes.

10. Contact Information

If the User has any questions about this Agreement, please contact the Provider at https://www.sysoit.com/.

By using the G-Contacts app, the User acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Personal Security Agreement.
